My Interests

Creative

Animation

I aspire to become a story board artist and writer. My biggest inspiration has been anime, Disney animation and Gaming. I took an interest in these because I had found myself to be drawn into their never ending universe of imagination.

It is their characters and stories which inspire me to work towards a greater goal in design and literature. Telling stories through art and expressing their beauty through it, has been my dream

Contumacious

Writer

I am the author of the 6 book novel called the Colossal Saga . The story is a journey brought to you from the hallowed halls of the realm of magic. This is the initial stage of my goal to becoming a great writer.

Elements of boldness, upheavals of emotions and arising joy is the feeling I have always wished to inculcate into my readers. P.S I also have a fan following

Illustrative

Story Writer

I am also the creator and illustrator of a comic named "C.H.I.N.U". It is a step inside a world, the likes of which you have never seen before. It accompanies the story of two rather drastically polar opposites and their adventures and misgivings on a universal scale.

It is a later plan to work on this to create an experience for everyone to enjoy, in form of an illustrated manga or a web-toon. I guarantee you that this will tickle your funny bone and leave you laughing, cringing and elevating your mood at the very least.
