I am a 20 year old aspiring storyboard artist and designer. A dabbling author, a cheerful and humourous story teller. I am the author of a 6 story novel series called "The Colossal Saga". A dark fantasy and adventure story which messes with the imagination and laws of nature. As well as the original script writer and illustrator of a lighthearted comic called CHINU.

My interests lie in playing the piano, listening to music, gaming, drawing and illustrating and writing the perfect novel.

I have been awarded runner up in SPIRITS Short story writing, first place in music showcase and have opened stalls for commissions in events.

My strengths lie in commitments and honesty in my work. I am eager to learn, no job is too big, no task too small. I am ready to take on anything. My weakness, in my health. i am proud and honoured to make your acquaintance
